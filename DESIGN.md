# DailyMon Design System

## 1. Design Concept
- **Theme**: Energetic, Cute, Gamified
- **Keywords**: Playful, Soft, Rounded, Motivating
- **Experience**: Like raising a digital pet while building real-life habits.

## 2. Color Palette
The color scheme uses soft pastels with a vibrant primary accent to maintain a fun yet readable atmosphere.

### Primary Colors
- **Mint Vitality** (Primary): `#30e87d` (Used for actions, progress bars, active states)
- **Soft Background**: `#F0FDF4` (Very pale mint/white for main background)
- **Card Background**: `#FFFFFF` (White)

### Accent Colors (Habit Categories)
- **Water Blue**: `#7CD6FD` (Hydration quests)
- **Step Orange**: `#FDBA7C` (Movement quests)
- **Mind Purple**: `#C47CFD` (Reading/Study quests)
- **Coin Gold**: `#FFD95A` (Currency/Rewards)

### Neutral Colors
- **Text Main**: `#2D3436` (Dark, soft charcoal for headings)
- **Text Body**: `#636E72` (Cool grey for secondary text)
- **Border/Divider**: `#EFEFEF`

## 3. Typography
- **Font Family**: 'Plus Jakarta Sans', sans-serif
- **Characteristics**: Geometric, rounded terminals, friendly.
- **Scale**:
  - **H1 (Level)**: 32px, ExtraBold (800)
  - **H2 (Section Titles)**: 24px, Bold (700)
  - **H3 (Card Titles)**: 18px, SemiBold (600)
  - **Body**: 14px-16px, Medium (500)

## 4. UI Components

### Shapes & Border Radius
- **Global Roundness**: `24px` to `32px` (Pill shapes and highly rounded cards).
- All buttons and logic cards should feel "squeezable" and soft.

### Shadows (Soft Depth)
- **Card Shadow**: `0px 8px 24px rgba(149, 157, 165, 0.1)`
- **Button Shadow**: `0px 4px 12px rgba(48, 232, 125, 0.3)`

### Icons & Imagery
- **Mascot**: 3D Rendered, round, cute monster component.
- **Icons**: Filled, colorful icons with rounded edges.

## 5. Layout (Mobile/Responsive)
- **Container**: Max-width 480px (Mobile-first approach), centered on desktop.
- **Spacing**: Generous padding (24px) to let elements breathe.
- **Navigation**: Bottom floating dock style.
